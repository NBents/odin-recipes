Simple Pasta

Description

This incredibly delicious pasta is just as tasty as it is simple to make! With only 5 ingredients needed, this has become a staple in our house. The paring of olive oil, red pepper flakes, and parmesan will transport your right back to your Nonna's house in Florence. All-in, this recipe will only take 25 minutes to make, leaving you with plently of time to have an adventure on a full stomach.

This recipe has been passed down from generation to generation in my family and although it is so simple, there are some tricks to making it POP! So, make sure that you read through this recipe and bring a little bit of Italy to your home no matter where you live!

Ingredients

  • 1lb - Angel Hair Pasta
  • 1/3 cup - Reserved Pasta Water
  • 1/4 cup - Olive Oil
  • 1 tbls - Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1/4 cup - Grated Parmesan
  • 1/4 cup - Chopped Fresh Parsley
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil 2 qts of water.
  2. Follow instructions on pasta box, cook pasta as instructed. Reserve pasta water for future step.
  3. Once pasta is done cooking. Turn burner to low. Add olive oil, pasta water, salt, pepper, and crushed red pepper flakes. Simmmer for 3 minutes.
  4. Add parmesan and parsley. Mix.
  5. Dish as desired.
